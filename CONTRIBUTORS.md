merged PR, a merged correction, or a substantive review.

## How to be added

After your first merged contribution:

1. Open a PR adding yourself to the relevant section.
2. Use initials, a handle, or a full name — your call. We will not infer.
3. Optionally include a link (personal site, GitHub profile).

Format:

```
- AS — [github.com/example](https://github.com/example) — verification calls, Bangalore
```

We will not list anyone without their PR or explicit written consent.
