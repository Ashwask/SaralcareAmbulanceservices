# Commercial Licensing

The Saralcare Ambulance Directory dataset is free for non-commercial
use under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 (see `LICENSE-DATA`).

**For-profit / commercial use requires a separate, bilateral license.**

This includes:

- Ingesting the dataset into a commercial product, app, API, or SaaS.
- Embedding the data in a service monetised by subscription,
  advertising, lead generation, paid placement, or transaction fees.
- Insurance claims processing, hospital chain proprietary systems,
  ambulance aggregator apps, and fleet-management products.
- Any redistribution gated by payment.

## How to request a commercial license

Email **`ashwinsk@saralcare.com`** with subject `[LICENSE REQUEST]` and
include:

1. **Your organisation:** legal name, registration, sector.
2. **Use case:** what you intend to build with the data.
3. **Integration scope:** internal use only, customer-facing product,
   API redistribution, white-label, etc.
4. **Expected volume:** approximate queries per month, geographic
   scope, expected user base.
5. **Attribution plan:** where and how Saralcare attribution will
   appear in your product (a public attribution clause is part of all
   standard commercial terms).
6. **Data-freshness expectations:** SLA on update frequency you would
   require.

## What to expect

- An acknowledgement within 48 hours.
- A bilateral quote, based on a private internal rubric, within 7
  working days.
- A standard license template, plus any negotiated terms.
- Signed bilaterally; license logged privately by the rights-holder.

There is **no public price list**. There is **no self-serve signup**.
There is **no tier marketing**. Each license is bespoke.

## What commercial licenses always include

- A public attribution clause: licensee's product/site must visibly
  credit "Saralcare ambulance directory (ambulance.saralcare.com)" in
  a manner agreed at signing. White-label is not granted in v1.
- A data-freshness SLA: licensee receives access to update deltas at
  the agreed cadence.
- Use-case scope: license is bound to the disclosed use case;
  expansion requires re-agreement.
- No sub-licensing or onward redistribution of the raw dataset.
- Indemnity scoped to the licensee's own use; the rights-holder
  accepts no liability for the licensee's product or its outcomes.

## What we will not license

- Use cases that conflict with the public-good positioning of the
  dataset — for example, locking competitor information behind a
  paywall in a way that prevents the public from reaching providers
  free of charge.
- White-labeling that removes attribution.
- Onward redistribution of the raw dataset to third parties.
- Use that would compromise our verification operation (e.g.,
  bulk-scraping of providers without verification cadence).

## Funds

Proceeds from commercial licensing, if any, are reinvested into the
verification operation — phone time, RTI fees, hosting, contributor
honoraria. Aggregate stats may be published once a meaningful number
of licenses exist; individual licensees are not named publicly without
their consent.
