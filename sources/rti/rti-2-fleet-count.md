I am applying under the Right to Information Act, 2005 for the following information related to the Arogya Kavacha — 108 EMS, operated by the Government of Karnataka under the National Health Mission. The information is requested for purposes of civic research and the maintenance of a public-good, openly licensed directory of emergency medical services in India.

(Reference: RTI application dated _______ regarding station list, acknowledgement number _______, if responded.)

The information sought is as follows:

1. **Fleet count per station.** For each 108 ambulance station in Karnataka, the number of ambulances assigned, as on the date this application is processed.

2. **Vehicle type breakdown.** For each station, the breakdown of the assigned fleet by vehicle type, using the following categories:
   - Basic Life Support (BLS).
   - Advanced Life Support (ALS).
   - Mobile Intensive Care Unit (MICU).
   - Patient Transport Ambulance (PTA).
   - Neonatal.
   - Any other category in operational use.

3. **Equipment standards.** The minimum equipment specification mandated for each vehicle type under the current 108 contract (oxygen, defibrillator, ventilator, cardiac monitor, suction, etc.).

4. **Staffing model.** For each vehicle type, the minimum staffing complement mandated under the current 108 contract (driver, EMT, paramedic, doctor onboard, etc.).

5. **Fleet age.** The average age of the 108 fleet in Karnataka and the count of vehicles older than 5 years and older than 10 years.
